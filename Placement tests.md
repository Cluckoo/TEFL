These are for determining the level of a student. Larger schools will have finer placement due to having more students.

Find placement tests: https://en.islcollective.com/english-esl-worksheets/search/diagnostic+test

If students have already done placement tests, they may not want another formal test. Here are some alternatives:
1. Getting-to-know-you activities
2. Pairs interview each other (speaking)
3. Listening/reading activity from coursebook. Collect answers to mark yourself.
4. Short writing task
